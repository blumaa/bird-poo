Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 10, 2026

Bird Poo is developed by Aaron Blum.

Data Collection

Bird Poo does not collect, store, or share any personal data.

Local Storage

The app uses local storage (UserDefaults on iOS) solely for saving game preferences such as high scores. This data never leaves your device.

Analytics

The iOS app does not use any analytics or tracking.

The web version of Bird Poo uses Vercel Analytics for anonymous usage statistics only. No personally identifiable information is collected.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, you can reach out at blumaa@gmail.com.
